Refath Bari

Refath Bari is a Physics BS student at CCNY with a 3.95 GPA. His numerical solution of the generalized Brachistochrone problem was published in the peer-reviewed journal The Physics Teacher. Refath is currently an exoplanet researcher under Dr. Amit Levi, a research associate at the Harvard Center for Astronomy. Their work on Nitrogen in Water Worlds will be submitted to The Astrophysical Journal in July 2023. Previously, he was an AI Researcher at the Meriles Lab at CCNY under Prof. Carlos Meriles. He has recently independently solved a variation of the moving mirrors problem. His solution is currently under peer review at The European Journal of Physics. Refath serves as a peer-review referee for The Physics Teacher (Reference: Editor Gary White, gwhite@gwu.edu).
